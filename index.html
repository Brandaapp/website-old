Branda

What it is

Branda is a mobile app that is a two-sided platform. It connects the students of Brandeis with essential campus services. These services include but are not limited to News, Shuttle Tracking, Laundry, Work Orders, Calendar, Hours, COVID-19 information etc. Branda is developed and maintained by Brandeis alumni and The Branda Team that consists of over twenty current undergraduate students.
